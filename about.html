Multiplex Street Parking started in a humble way in 2003 when it was contracted by Kampala City Council (KCC) to manage and control Street Parking in Kampala.
In 2010, we were contracted by Kampala Capital City Authority to Manage and Modernise Street Parking with the objective of procuring & deploying the technologies below;

  • Pay and Display parking meters.
  • Hand held meters - Point of Sale Machines (POS).
  • Automated Number plate recognition System.
  • The parking devices were hiked to the central server
    which facilitated monitoring of parking operations.

In 2017, following a review of parking technologies in use, it was determined that P&D Meters were not suitable for our environment where voluntary compliance among
motorists is low and network coverage is poor leading to unstable connectivity.
The ANPRS mounted on pick-ups were also not very effective in capturing Motor vehicle number plates for vehicles parked bumper to bumper.
The review revealed that the most effective technology would be Android POS Machines with real time connectivity to Central Server.

In 2017/ 2018, KCCA revised the Contract and required that the parking management system in use be revamped and realigned to the ideals of Smart City.
Currently we use the POS machines which are used for invoicing and also to detect defaulting vehicles and notify Enforcement Personnel providing vehicle number and its physical location.
